How Paper Trading Works on Alpha Investing

Orders are simulated using live market data so that you can learn execution without risking real money.

1 · Set capital

Choose your virtual bankroll

Start with virtual ₹1L / ₹5L / ₹10L and see how your process performs over 50–100 trades.

2 · Place orders

Use real-style tickets

Market and (later) limit / SL orders are simulated using live quotes from Finnhub.

3 · Review

Analyse your behaviour

Track winners, losers, time in trade and risk per idea before touching real capital.

  1. Build a routine: review markets, plan trades, then execute what matches your plan.
  2. Focus on process, not rupee P&L at the beginning.
  3. Graduate to real money only after a stable sample of successful paper trades.
